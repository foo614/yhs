# Product

## Register

product

## Users

YS Heng staff use this portal during daily vehicle sales operations: management, sales, repair, loan, delivery, finance, HR, and admin users. They are usually checking live work, following up exceptions, and moving records between departments.

## Product Purpose

The back office keeps vehicle stock, customer leads, loans, deliveries, repairs, payments, settlement reminders, audit logs, and staff access in one operational system. Success means staff can see what needs action, trust the numbers, and update the right record without hunting.

## Brand Personality

Calm, practical, accountable. The product should feel like a reliable operations desk for a vehicle business, not a marketing page or generic analytics demo.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative SaaS dashboards, oversized hero sections, generic gradient cards, glass effects, repeated identical metric grids, and vague AI-generated copy. Avoid UI that hides workflow exceptions behind pretty charts.

## Design Principles

- Put urgent operational exceptions before decorative summaries.
- Keep dashboard density high enough for experienced staff to scan quickly.
- Use familiar Ant Design controls and table behavior so staff do not need to relearn the portal.
- Make finance, stock, and workflow risk visibly different without over-coloring inactive data.
- Prefer specific vehicle-sales labels over broad business dashboard language.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable contrast, keyboard-friendly standard controls, responsive layouts that do not overlap, and reduced reliance on color alone for status meaning.
